CloudSky is a Multipurpose Domain, Hosting and WHMCS Template designed for All kinds of Technology, Domain and Hosting Business. It's an HTML5 template based on Bootstrap 3.3.7. Also included WHMCS Template based on WHMCS v7.7. Anyone can easily update/edit this plugin to follow our Well Sorted Documentation.

Mainly, our template is an HTML template and we just provided support by customizing WHMCS header and footer and color based on our template for FREE of cost with our HTML template.
